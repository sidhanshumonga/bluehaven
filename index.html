About Us

Blue Haven is an innovative concept aimed at providing the privacy of flats at affordable prices with customizable furnishing to let you create your own home.

Affordable

The barrier of unreasonable prices is no longer a problem. Get 1 BHK, 2 BHK or 1 RK homes furnished to your liking at affordable prices.

Customizable

With furnished and semi-furnished flats, Blue Haven lets you personalize your abode to make it as close to home as possible.

Privacy

We provide you places with the comforts of a PG and the privacy of flat. This means you get your basic necessities like food and housekeeping sorted, and have the complete privacy of living in your own flat.
